Live Traveling

Target Audience

A website intended for all those adventurous individuals or groups of people, even families, who want to see new places, ancient and unique places of the world with an exquisite culture and have edifying and rewarding activities that will accompany them for the rest of their life. This colorful website serves as a first glimpse of what awaits you in this beautiful city.

Personas

Rodriguez family

The Rodriguez family goes on vacation every year to visit new places, they are a large family with different personalities, so they need to go to a diverse place that suits the whole family, they decided to visit our website "Live Traveling" to make the decision of their next tourist destination..

Costa group

It is an organization formed by leaders who program outings, activities and trips. Grupo Costa organizes outings at different times of the year within the city, no matter the weather. Don't be fooled, the tours are very fun and full of stories and charisma provided by this organization helps to spread the culture to all those who wish to know it. In their travel programs, before they start, they give visitors a first glimpse based on information from the "Live traveling" website.
