Target Audience

Traders and learning and trade organizations, help people to search their intereses and general public NGOs with related interests in starting a business, families and individuals interested in learning and learning about the growth of the organization. from all CABA and AMBA

Personas

Martin

He has a family. Martin is a 36 years old is a father of 3. he works from home which allows him to be present for his children. Martin likes to spend time alone with his wife walking around the city. He will use the website to search for places to take his wife for dinner.

Emma Jhonson

Emma is a 43 years old and mother of 2. She likes carpentry as a way to be self-sufficient and in this way he wants to teach his children the trade and at the same time spend time with them while working as a family.

Scenario

Martin consults the website announcements and news for ideas on where to take the family for a weekend outing

Emma enters the website where she searches for handmade art events to buy and sell what she creates.

Martin goes to play soccer outdoors with his childs after school. They arrive at the field and find that it is occupied, so Martin remembers that on the BUENOS AIRES COMMERCE website there is a guide and map to see where the nearest field is to go and play.

Martin wants to give something special to his wife for her birthday but he is not very creative, so he searches in buenos aires commerce for ideas and gifts to surprise her.

Emma wants to improve her business and become better at woodworking, so she went to the Buenos Aires trade website to look for courses to help her improve.
