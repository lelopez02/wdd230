Typography

For titles H1, H2, H3:
For the options menu in the top bar, we will use white to make it stand out from the background. For the headings that will make up the body of the page and introduce specific topics such as weather, news, etc. we will use black because the background is white. In both cases we will use Source Sans Pro font.

For paragraph and navigation:
For the paragraphs, and the display of information as well as the navigation links we will use the Popins font making its colors alternate between black and white depending on the contrast and color of the background.
