Santa Monica is a coastal city west of downtown Los Angeles. Santa Monica Beach is surrounded by Palisades Park, overlooking the Pacific Ocean. On the Santa Monica Pier, you will find the Pacific Park amusement park, the Looff Hippodrome Carousel and the Santa Monica Pier Aquarium. Adjacent to the pier is Muscle Beach, an outdoor gymnasium established in the 1930s. Downtown is Bergamot Station, which houses several art galleries.
